# Source Notes: conways-law

- Primary source: How Do Committees Invent?
- Author: Melvin E. Conway
- Year: 1968
- URL: https://www.melconway.com/Home/Committees_Paper.html

## Key Concepts

- System designs tend to mirror the communication structures that produce them.
- Boundaries need maintainers, reviewers, deployers, and consumers who can sustain them.
- A technically clean split can fail when ownership and coordination do not match it.

## Agent Translation

Use it to map ownership and handoffs before changing architecture, packages, or service seams.

## Common Misuse

Do not preserve poor structure forever just because the current organization produced it.

## Source Mapping
