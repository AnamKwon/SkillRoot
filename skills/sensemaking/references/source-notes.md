# Source Notes: sensemaking

- Primary source: Sensemaking in Organizations
- Author: Karl E. Weick
- Year: 1995
- URL: https://www.sagepub.com/shop/buy-a-book/sensemaking-in-organizations-1-4988
- Supporting source: Sensemaking in Organizations: Reflections on Karl Weick and Social Theory
- Supporting URL: https://www.epicpeople.org/sensemaking-in-organizations/

## Key Concepts

- Understanding is cue-driven, retrospective, and provisional.
- A useful story is plausible and testable, not prematurely certain.
- New evidence should update the narrative instead of being forced into it.

## Agent Translation

Use it to gather cues, build a working story, and choose a diagnostic action that could change the story.

## Common Misuse

Do not label a cause like cache issue or race condition without supporting cues.

## Source Mapping

Cues -> plausible story -> diagnostic test -> revised story.
